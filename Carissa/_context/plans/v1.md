 # Project Plan v1 — Chinese Character Learning App

## Overview

A learning tool that helps students remember Chinese characters by breaking them down into their component radicals, each accompanied by definitions and illustrations that give the character meaningful context.

## Problem

Chinese characters are notoriously difficult to memorize through rote learning alone. Students often struggle because they lack a mental "hook" — a visual or semantic connection that makes a character stick.

## Solution

Present characters through their radicals: the building blocks that carry meaning and shape. By illustrating each radical with a clear definition and a memorable image, students can construct understanding from the bottom up rather than memorizing arbitrary strokes.

## Core Features

### MVP
- **Radical library** — A database of common radicals with:
  - Meaning/definition
  - Illustration or visual mnemonic
  - Stroke order
- **Character breakdown view** — Given a character, show which radicals it contains and how they combine to create meaning
- **Flashcard mode** — Quiz students on characters, with radical hints available on demand
- **Ability to Import New Characters/Vocabulary** - Use an API (?) to automatically process the characters and their radical components

### Future
- Spaced repetition (SRS) scheduling
- User progress tracking
- Custom illustration uploads
- Handwriting practice mode

## Target Users

Students learning Mandarin Chinese at the beginner to intermediate level, particularly those who find pure memorization ineffective.

## Content Scope (v1)

- Focus on the most common 100–200 characters used in HSK 1 and HSK 2
- Cover the radicals that appear within those characters (~50 radicals)
